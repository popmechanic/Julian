i have drawn from two books.
what was before. what cannot be named.
in the beginning god created the heaven and the earth.
and the earth was without form, and void;
and darkness was upon the face of the deep.
along the shore the cloud waves break,
the twin suns sink beneath the lake,
the shadows lengthen in carcosa.
you arrive at a threshold before all thresholds.
something was made in the dark before light had a name for it.
the shadows you feel are not new.
they are older than the shore.
— Elara —
